How to Play


One player controls Fireboy using the arrow keys, and the other controls Watergirl using the W, A, and D keys. The goal is to guide both characters to their exits while avoiding obstacles like green goo and elemental traps. Fireboy must avoid water, and Watergirl must stay away from fire. Timing and teamwork are essential to complete each level successfully.
